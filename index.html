Instructions

You have a random number as you targetin the Crystal section

At the begining of the game you have score of Zero. Each time, when you click the crystal, the value of the crystal you get will be added in your score.

Your goal is the match the target value. If you macth the target value, you will win and if you crossed the target value, you will lose the game.
